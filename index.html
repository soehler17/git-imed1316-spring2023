Our Story

Texas Coffee was founded in the Hill Country of the Lone Star State. We've always loved the taste of coffee on an early Hill Country morning. Watching the sunrise and drinking coffee, we got to thinking that maybe we could make our own. And that's where it all started.

We really started to get into the process. From going to retreats and different roasting facilities to learn the basics of just how you bring this little green beans and turn it into brown water. Once we got the basics down, we found some green coffee beans that a local roasting facility had and we started to experiment.

After doing all of that, we wanted to know more. So we packed our bags and traveled to South America. Colombia to be exact. We started to learn more about the natural ways that the Latin Americans roast their coffee. They taught us the ins and outs of coffee roasting and showed up what little was needed to do so.

From then on, we've been experimenting on our owns and we've also been successful in getting some friends attached to the coffee world as well. We started to buy roasting equipment that wasn't too pricey and find the coffee that we just so longed for. After using the cheaper stuff and profitting off of it, we decided to upgrade our equipment. We did that so that we could give you the best coffee you've ever had. We get all of our beans from Colombia, Brazil, and Ethopia, just to name a few. We strive to give you the best so you don't have to get that other cheap made coffee. And we offer it at a darn good price too!
